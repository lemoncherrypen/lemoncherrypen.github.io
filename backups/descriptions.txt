*What are complex features? Examples: detailed tattoos, props, detailed/multiple accessories, mecha.


**What is a complex background? The price for a complex background will usually be $3 added to the base price of the artwork. Examples of $3 backgrounds: cities, forests, bedrooms, basically anything a step up from solid color and simple pattern backgrounds. Prices may vary when backgrounds are densely packed and extremely detailed, or where a character is not the focus of the artwork.


What is the difference between a headshot and a half body? If the picture focuses on the head and shoulders, it's a headshot. If the picture includes the character's chest or arms, it will count as a half body. Then it becomes a full body if the legs are included.
