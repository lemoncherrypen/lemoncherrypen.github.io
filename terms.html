FAQ

Send me an email at lemoncherrypen@gmail.com with a general idea of what you'd like and any reference pictures you'd like to include. You can also message me on Instagram @lemoncherrypen. Feel free to message me with general questions or to ask for a quote as well.

Examples of complex features are detailed tattoos, props, detailed/multiple accessories, mecha. Complex features will add $1-2 to the base price of a commission.

The price for a complex background will usually be $3 added to the base price of the artwork. Examples of $3 backgrounds: cities, forests, bedrooms, basically anything a step up from solid color and simple pattern backgrounds. Prices may vary when backgrounds are densely packed and extremely detailed, or where a character is not the focus of the artwork.

These commissions are digital. If you would like to purchase it as a physical copy, we can discuss an extra charge to help with the cost of supplies, packaging, and shipping.

You can pay with Paypal or Venmo. Once we agree on what your commission will look like, I will need at least a partial payment upfront in order to get started. You may either pay 50% upfront and the rest upon completion, or pay full price upfront. Either way, I will send you at least one progress shot for your approval and a chance to suggest changes once I complete it. I can make minor changes to the final product up to three times for free. After that, additional changes will cost $1.
