Terms & FAQ

Commissioning me means that you accept the conditions outlined on this page.

FAQ

Send me an email at lemoncherrypen@gmail.com with a general idea of what you'd like and any reference pictures you'd like to include. You can also message me on Instagram @lemoncherrypen. Feel free to message me with general questions or to ask for a quote as well.

You can pay with Paypal or Venmo. Once we agree on what your commission will look like, I will need at least a partial payment upfront in order to get started. You may either pay 50% upfront and the rest upon completion, or pay full price upfront. Either way, I will send you at least one progress shot for your approval on the design, and a chance to suggest changes once I complete it. When I receive the full payment, I'll send you the fully rendered image. I can make minor changes to the final product up to three times for free. After that, additional changes will cost $3.

Examples of complex features are detailed tattoos, props, detailed/multiple accessories, mecha. Complex features will add $1-2 to the base price of a commission.

Examples of complex backgrounds: cities, forests, bedrooms, basically anything a step up from solid color and simple pattern backgrounds. Prices may vary when backgrounds are densely packed and extremely detailed.

These commissions are digital only.

1-2 weeks. After we've talked about what you want the commission to look like and I receive at least 50% of the payment upfront, I can begin working on your art (this is when the 1-2 weeks estimate begins). The time it takes will vary depending on the complexity and how much back and forth messaging there is for design changes. I don't offer any "rushed delivery" options for finishing it faster at this time, so please plan on it taking 2 weeks at the most.

There are only two cases where I can issue a refund:

  1. I am unable to finish the work for some reason. In this case, you have the option of either a full refund, or sometimes the option of a reduced price for a picture that ended up smaller/uncolored.
  2. You gave me a 100% payment upfront but then changed your mind. In this case, I can only refund 50% because I have already put time into working on it.
If you gave me the non-refundable 50% upfront and then are unable to give me the remaining half, just let me know. I will then stop working on it and just not send you the completed image.

  • You may not sell the artwork. If you, for example, wanted your design on a shirt, feel free to make one for personal use. But you can't sell the design in any way.
  • These are digital commissions. If you would like it printed, I don't offer printing services, but feel free to get it printed somewhere else for personal use.
  • I have the right to deny any commission request for any reason.
  • I only offer refunds in specific situations. The 50% downpayment is non-refundable unless I can't finish your commission.
  • I will not begin working on the commission until I receive at least 50% payment upfront.
  • I will not send the completed commission to you until I receive the rest of the payment due.
  • You may ask for minor changes to be made to the final product up to three times. After that, each change will cost $3.
