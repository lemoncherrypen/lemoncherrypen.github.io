Terms & FAQ

Commissioning me means that you accept the conditions outlined on this page.

Send me an email at lemoncherrypen@gmail.com with a general idea of what you'd like and any reference pictures you'd like to include. You can also message me on Instagram @lemoncherrypen. Feel free to message me with general questions or to ask for a quote.

Paypal or Venmo. Once we agree on what your commission will look like, I will ask for a 50% payment upfront to get started on your project.

Examples of complex features are detailed tattoos, props, detailed/multiple accessories, mecha. Complex features will add $1-2 to the base price of a commission.

Examples of complex backgrounds: cities, forests, bedrooms, basically anything a step up from solid color and simple pattern backgrounds. Prices may vary when backgrounds are densely packed and extremely detailed.

Yeah there are some requests I don't accept for commissions. There are plenty of things I'm open to drawing, such as human characters, furries, armor, fantasy species, and certain types of mature content. I will not draw things for commercial use, and I have restrictions on mature content. For example, one of my rules is that any character depicted in NSFW art must be an adult and may not be an aged-up version of a minor. There is no guaruntee that I will accept a request, because sometimes I'm just not comfortable drawing something.
If you have a request but are not sure if I will draw it, feel free to send me a message on social media, or email me at lemoncherrypen@gmail.com. Please respect that I have the right to deny any commission request for any reason.

These commissions are digital only.

About 1-2 weeks. Time to complete the project will depend on its complexity. I will only begin working on it after I've received the initial 50% payment. I'll keep you in the loop with how your project is going by sending you progress pictures for your approval.

There are only two cases where I can issue a refund:

  1. I am unable to finish the work for some reason. In this case, you have the option of either a full refund, or sometimes the option of a reduced price for a picture that ended up smaller/uncolored.
  2. You gave me a 100% payment upfront but then changed your mind about wanting the commission. In this case, I can only refund 50% because I have already put time into working on it.
If you gave me the non-refundable 50% upfront and then are unable to give me the remaining half, just let me know. In this case, we can cancel the commission or see if another payment plan would work better.

Summary

  • You may not sell the artwork. If you, for example, wanted your design on a shirt, feel free to make one for personal use. But you can't sell the design in any way.
  • These are digital commissions. If you would like it printed, I don't offer printing services, but feel free to get it printed somewhere else for personal use.
  • I have the right to deny any commission request for any reason.
  • I only offer refunds in specific situations. The 50% downpayment is non-refundable unless I can't finish your commission.
  • I will not begin working on the commission until I receive at least 50% payment upfront.
  • I will not send the completed commission to you until I receive the rest of the payment due.
  • You may ask for minor changes to be made to the final product up to three times. After that, each change will cost $3.
